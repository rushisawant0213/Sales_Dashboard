# Mobile Sales Dashboard

## Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of mobile phone sales performance. It helps businesses monitor revenue, sales volume, customer transactions, and city-wise sales distribution through interactive visualizations.

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### Total Sales
- Displays the total revenue generated from mobile phone sales.
- Helps track overall business performance.

### Total Quantity
- Shows the total number of mobile phones sold.
- Measures product demand and sales volume.

### Total Transactions
- Represents the total number of completed sales transactions.
- Helps analyze customer purchasing activity.

### Average Price
- Displays the average selling price of mobile phones.
- Calculated as:
  
  Average Price = Total Sales / Total Quantity

### Total Sales by City
- Shows revenue generated across different cities.
- Helps identify top-performing markets and regional sales trends.

## Features
- Interactive KPI Cards
- City-wise Sales Analysis
- Sales Performance Tracking
- Transaction Analysis
- Average Price Monitoring
- Dynamic Filters and Slicers

## Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Microsoft Excel / CSV Dataset

## Business Insights
- Identifies top-performing cities.
- Tracks overall sales growth.
- Analyzes customer purchasing patterns.
- Evaluates product demand across locations.
- Supports data-driven business decisions.
